Project Purpose

Turning textbook theorems into visual learning

This project is designed to help students understand geometry and circle theorems through movement, sequence, and simple reasoning instead of only memorizing textbook proofs.

Problem Statement

Many students find theorems difficult because book proofs are static and use mathematical words that feel hard to understand. This platform breaks each theorem into guided steps, easier words, and animated diagrams.

Learning Features

  • Chapter-wise theorem organization for Class 9 and Class 10
  • Animated SVG diagrams with highlighted proof steps
  • Student-friendly explanation and easy word meanings
  • Given, To Prove, Construction, and Final Result sections

How To Use

1. Choose a class

Start with Class 9 or Class 10 according to your syllabus.

2. Select a chapter

Use the chapter cards to focus on one topic at a time.

3. Open a theorem

Read the theorem statement, purpose, and construction summary.

4. Learn step by step

Use Next Step or Auto Play to follow the diagram animation and logic.
